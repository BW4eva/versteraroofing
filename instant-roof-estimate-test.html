Local Roofing Experts Serving Central Texas

Green Shield Roofing provides professional roof inspections, maintenance, repair, replacement and storm damage services throughout Central Texas and the Austin, San Antonio, Killeen/Temple metropolitan areas. We understand how Central Texas homes face intense sun exposure, high winds, and frequent hailstorms.

We commonly see aging pipe boots, deteriorating sealants, builder-grade flashing, ventilation issues, and storm-related shingle damage on Central Texas roofs— often before leaks are visible from inside the home.

Our approach is proactive and educational. Whether you need preventative maintenance, leak repairs, or help after a hailstorm, we provide clear answers—not sales pressure.

What should I do if my roof has storm damage?

If your roof has storm damage, don't worry – we're here to help! We provide free storm damage inspections and assist with insurance claims from start to finish. Contact us immediately to schedule an inspection, and we'll document the damage and work directly with your insurance company to ensure you get the coverage you deserve.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential shingle roof replacements are completed within 1 day, depending on the size of your home, and weather conditions. If you desire an upgrade to hail resistant materials, like Stone-Coated Steel metal roofing which can deliver tens of thousands of dollars in lifetime savings, those projects typically take 3-5 days to complete.

What types of roofing materials do you install?

We specialize in metal roofing, premium hail-resistant asphalt shingles, tile roofing, and stone-coated steel. Each material offers unique benefits in terms of durability, energy efficiency, and aesthetics. Our team will help you choose the best option for your home and budget.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! We offer flexible financing options to make your roof replacement affordable. Contact us to learn more about our current financing programs and payment plans.
