Benefits of Annual Roof Maintenance

Prevent Roof Leaks Before They Start

Early detection of minor issues like loose fasteners or failing sealants prevents water infiltration that can lead to expensive interior damage and repairs.

Extend Your Roof's Lifespan

Regular maintenance can add years to your roof's life by addressing wear and tear before it compounds into major deterioration. Our client's roof typially last 3-5 years longer than the average Central Texas shingle roof when we conduct routine annual maintenance.

Protect Your Home's Interior

Routine maintenance of your roof, particularly re-sealing around all roof vents, accessories, and penetrations will prevent water leaks into your attic. This prevents wood rot and mold/mildew growth inside your home.

Reduce Emergency Repair Costs

Catching small problems during routine maintenance costs far less than emergency repairs after a leak has caused significant damage.

Peace of Mind

Know that your roof is professionally inspected and maintained, protecting your family and your investment throughout the year.

Annual Roof Maintenance FAQs

What exactly is included in the $149 maintenance service?

Our $149 annual maintenance includes a comprehensive roof inspection covering all roofing materials, flashing, penetrations, and drainage areas. We perform preventative maintenance including tightening loose fasteners, resealing exposed areas, and removing debris. You'll receive photo documentation and a detailed written report of our findings and recommendations. Minor repairs that can be completed quickly are included in the service.

How often should I have my roof inspected?

We recommend inspecting your roof at least once every couple years, and ALWAYS immediately after a severe hail or wind storm. To make things easy for our clients, we bundle our inspection services within our annual roof maintenance plan.

How often should I have my roof maintained?

We recommend annual roof maintenance for most homes, typically performed in the fall or winter. However, if your roof is older (15+ years), located in an area prone to severe weather, or has a history of issues, you may benefit from twice-yearly inspections. We can provide personalized recommendations based on your specific roof and circumstances.

Is this a contract or subscription service?

No, our $149 annual maintenance is a one-time service with no contracts or auto-renewals. You schedule the service when you want it, and there's no obligation for future services. Many of our customers choose to schedule maintenance annually, but it's completely at your discretion.

What if you find issues that need repair during maintenance?

If we identify issues during your maintenance visit, we'll document them thoroughly and provide you with a clear explanation and honest recommendation. Minor repairs that take minimal time and materials are often included in the $149 service. For more extensive repairs, we'll provide a separate estimate with no pressure or obligation. You're always in control of what work gets done.
