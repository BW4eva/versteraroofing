About Yesterday's Gone:

Yesterday’s Gone is a transitional housing program for abused and traumatized women, their children, and pets. Each woman can live in their own tiny home for up to 12 months, and receive full-time life coaching, weekly counseling, transportation, employment assistance, and have access to local resources. We believe no one should have to go back to an abusive situation.

How we are partnering:

For every full roof replacement job we complete in the month of October, we will donate half of the profits to Yesterday's Gone to support victims of Domestic Violence.
