Overview

This page confirms that Verstera Roofing is a secure, encrypted website that protects all customer data, uses HTTPS/SSL, employs Cloudflare firewall protection, encrypts form submissions, uses Google reCAPTCHA, stores no customer information on the website server, and does not sell or share data. All personal information is used only for contacting clients regarding roofing services. All insurance documents, photos, and estimates are securely handled and only shared with homeowners.

Your Privacy & Security Matter to Us

At Verstera Roofing , your trust is our top priority. Whether you’re requesting a free roof inspection or submitting documentation for an insurance claim, you can count on us to protect your personal information with industry-leading security standards.

This page explains — in plain language — how we keep your information safe, secure, and private.

1. Secure Website & Encrypted Connection

Your entire experience on our website is protected with:

  • ✔ HTTPS encryption
  • ✔ Verified SSL Certificate
  • ✔ Cloudflare security & firewall protection
  • ✔ DNS protection & bot filtering

This ensures everything you send through our site is encrypted from end to end.

2. Secure Forms & Data Protection

Every form submission is protected by multiple layers:

  • ✔ Encrypted data transmission
  • ✔ Google reCAPTCHA to block spam & malicious bots
  • ✔ No data stored on our website servers
  • ✔ Submissions routed directly to our secure CRM & email

We never store sensitive customer data on our website hosting environment. This reduces risk and ensures your information cannot be accessed through unauthorized channels.

3. Your Information Is Never Shared or Sold

Verstera Roofing, LLC follows a strict privacy standard:

  • We never sell, rent, or share customer information.
  • Information is used only to contact you about roofing inspections, storm-damage help, or services you request.
  • We do not engage in unsolicited marketing or third-party data sharing.
  • You may request to have your data deleted at any time.

4. Secure Handling of Insurance Claim Documents

  • ✔ Photos, estimates, adjuster reports, and documents are kept confidential
  • ✔ Files are shared only with you, and only when necessary to assist with your claim
  • ✔ We do not upload insurance documents to our website
  • ✔ All files are handled through secure systems

Your insurance information stays between you and us — period.
