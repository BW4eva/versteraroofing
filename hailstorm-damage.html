Signs Your Roof Has Hail Damage

  • ✓ Missing or damaged shingles exposing the roof deck
  • ✓ Dents or dimples in shingles, flashing, or gutters
  • ✓ Granule loss from asphalt shingles (check gutters and downspouts)
  • ✓ Soft spots or bruising when pressing on shingles
  • ✓ Water leaks or stains on ceilings or walls inside your home
  • ✓ Cracked or broken roof tiles (for tile roofs)

Don't see these signs but concerned about damage? Our experts can detect hidden hail damage that's not visible from the ground.
